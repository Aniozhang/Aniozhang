Orthogonal Projection

This work presents a straightforward U-Net model integrated with spatial transformations to convert an RGBD image of an arbitrary view of a table into an orthogonal projection. This approach significantly reduces setup time and costs in robotic experiments by eliminating the need for multiple depth cameras.

Efficient Inverse Kinematics for High-Dof Robots: A Kolmogrove-Arnold Network Approach

This research proposes KAN to estimate the output of the Inverse Kinematics algorithm for robotics. Among all the soft computing methods tested, KANs performed the best among all metrics. This work was based on Dr. Ziming Liu's work.
